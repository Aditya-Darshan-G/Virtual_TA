Module: Large Language Models Q5: Tokenization in NLP In Natural Language Processing, what is the primary purpose of tokenization? A. To translate text from one language to another. B. To split text into individual words or subwords. C. To encrypt text for secure communication. D. To summarize large texts into shorter versions. Answer : B — Tokenization involves breaking down text into smaller components, such as words or subwords, which can then be processed by language models. This is a fundamental step in NLP tasks.
